EVENT DETAILS


GROUP A

All Students of schools and colleges can participate.Each student can submit maximum 5 photographs 30 selected photographs will be exhibited from this group 3 best photographs will be awarded

Theme: Your shot (Open)



GROUP B

University Students & Professional PhotographersAll students of university level (including National University & Medical Colleges) & Freelancer around Bangladesh can participate in this group.

Each student can submit maximum 5 photographs50 selected photographs will be exhibited.Best 3 photographs will be awarded

Theme:

  • Emotion
  • Travel
  • Conceptual (manipulation is allowed)



GROUP C

Anyone participate in this group.Each participant can submit maximum 2 entries.Each story/ Series contains minimum 5 photos & maximum 8 photos.5 selected stories/series will be exhibited.1 story/Series will be awarded.

Theme: Open



SUBMISSION PROCEDURE

  • A participant can submit to maximum 2 groups. One is from Group A/B and other is Group C.

  • Each participant can submit a maximum of 5 photographs from their group in terms of single photographs and in case of series/story each can submit minimum 5 to maximum 8 photographs.

  • Submission of photographs exhibited previously in any national exhibition will be disqualified.

  • Both color and B&W photographs are allowed.

  • Only minor burning, dodging and color correction is acceptable. No Borders/ Watermarks/ Signatures/ logos are allowed.

  • Manipulation is strictly prohibited (Except Conceptual category of Group B).

  • Only digital submission is allowed. Photographs have to be submitted through following

  • Renaming (Important): Photo No_ Photographer's full name _Contact no
    For example: 01_Tamim Hossain _0171*******’

  • Entries must be in jpg or jpeg format and file size can be maximum 4 MB. Larger files above 4MB will not be accepted.


Registration Procedure

Selected photographers will have to submit a registration fee for exhibition.


  • Group A- 820 tk (Per Photograph)

  • Group B- 1,020 tk (Per Photograph)

  • Group C- 2,550 tk (Per Photograph)
